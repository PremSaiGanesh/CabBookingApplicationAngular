Introduction

The INdiGO Cab app offers mobility solutions by connecting customers to drivers and a wide range of vehicles across bikes, auto-rickshaws, metered taxis, and cabs, enabling convenience and transparency for consumers and driver-partners. IndiGO cabs was founded in April 2021 with a mission to build mobility for a billion people.
